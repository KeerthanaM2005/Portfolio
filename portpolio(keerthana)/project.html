Hospital Management System

About the Project

A Hospital Management System (HMS) is a software solution designed to streamline hospital operations, including patient management, appointments, billing, and staff administration. It enhances efficiency, reduces paperwork, and ensures better healthcare service delivery through digital record-keeping and automation.

Virtual Assistant Chatbot

About the Chatbot

A Virtual Assistant Chatbot is an AI-powered software that simulates human-like conversations to assist users with tasks, queries, and services. It utilizes natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning to provide automated, intelligent, and interactive responses across various platforms.
